Angela's Recipe for the Best cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 1/4teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1 cup butter
  • 3 cup bread flour

for the flling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instrunctions

  1. mix the milk with the yeast,sugar, eggs,
  2. melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
  4. knead the dough for 10 minuites.
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with platic wrap. leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours.
  6. after the dough has double in size ,roll it out into a large rectangle.
  7. Melt the butter for the filling and mix in the sigar and cinnamon.
  8. cut the roll into 3cm section and place flat bake the rolls for 20-50min lightly brown.
